Visit Rehoboth Beach, Delaware

Experience Delaware's most visited city. Warm sunshine, sandy beaches & ocean breezes. Rehoboth Beach welcomes you!

Top three activities to do while at Rehoboth Beach!

Beach and Boardwalk

It's what sun worshippers and beach bums come for: the shore. Beachgoers say the sands are clean and the waves are typically calm, making it very family-friendly. The beach is backed by a mile-long boardwalk that's packed with shops, restaurants and bars.

Funland

Family-owned and operated since 1962, this amusement park is a popular staple on the Rehoboth Beach Boardwalk. Featuring 19 rides, 16 midway games and an arcade, the old-fashioned park is perfect for kids (and parents) who need a break from the beach.

Tanger Outlets

If you tire of playing in the sand, you can indulge in some retail therapy less than 3 miles from the boardwalk at the Tanger Outlets. Home to more than 100 stores and restaurants, including shopper favorites like Coach, Vera Bradley, Nike and Old Navy.
